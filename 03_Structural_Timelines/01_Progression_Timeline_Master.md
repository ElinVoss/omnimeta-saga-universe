## OVERVIEW

This document provides a comprehensive timeline of the OmniMeta Saga across all 30 novellas, organized by **Turning** (5-novella arcs). Each entry includes:

- Crisis escalations
- Relationship developments
- Soulpulse capacity changes
- God-vessel faction activities

## TURNING 1: GLASS (N1–N5)

### N01: Grip That Holds

**Major Events:**
- The Piercing occurs (Ch.3) — projectile tears through Te'Oga
- Soulpulse awakens — magic becomes possible for first time
- Jhace's first Wholeness use (instinctive, saves injured child)
- Tiffani proposes partnership after witnessing Jhace's skill
- Chloen becomes first apprentice
- Infrastructure stabilized temporarily

**Character Arcs:**
- Jhace: Isolated worker → Partner → Teacher → Lover

**Soulpulse Capacity:**
- Jhace: Wholeness (primary), Form (learning from Tiffani), dual-principle emerging

**Crisis Level:** Catastrophic (Piercing) → Stabilizing

**Relationship Status:**
- Jhace + Tiffani: Partnership formed, romance solidified, committed
