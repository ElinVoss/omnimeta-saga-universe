# GOD-VESSEL & FACTION TIMELINE

**Document Version**: 1.0  
**Created**: October 31, 2025  
**Purpose**: Track god-vessel awakening, faction formation, and escalation across all novellas  
**Usage**: Reference for Perplexity Space when generating interlude content and faction activities  

---

## AWAKENING (N5)

All four god-vessels awaken simultaneously during Steal Breaks Open:

- **Xilcore/Corlexi** (Form) — Awakens in Shatterholm
- **Leesa/Salee** (Wholeness) — Awakens in Forgeport
- **Blemo/Mobel** (Purity) — Awakens in Cauldron-Hold
- **Seeri/Eries** (Entropy) — Awakens in Velocity-Heights

**Trigger**: Jhace's grief-driven four-principle explosion creates resonance that transforms four mortals into divine embodiments.

---

## XILCORE/CORLEXI (FORM PRINCIPLE)

**Doctrine**: Perfection through geometric correction  
**Faction Name**: Corlexi  
**Territory**: Shatterholm + expanding  

**Timeline**:
- **N5**: Awakens, begins geometric "corrections"
- **N6**: Faction forms, followers attracted to perfection doctrine
- **N7**: Builds first geometric strongholds
- **N11**: First confrontation with Jhace, attempts to "correct" his paradox
- **N13**: Orveth (lieutenant) builds fortifications
- **N15–N25**: Siege warfare, geometric weapons deployed
- **N26**: Orveth dies (fortress collapses)
- **N29**: Trapped in fivefold stasis
- **N30**: Learns orchestration truth, ceases

---

## LEESA/SALEE (WHOLENESS PRINCIPLE)

**Doctrine**: Compassion through forced integration  
**Faction Name**: Salee  
**Territory**: Forgeport + expanding  

**Timeline**:
- **N5**: Awakens, begins "healing" through coercion
- **N6**: Faction forms, followers seek belonging
- **N7**: Forced integration operations begin
- **N11**: Claims Jhace as "chosen vessel"
- **N14**: Thessa (lieutenant) escalates forced healing
- **N15–N25**: Integration campaigns, resistance members targeted
- **N27**: Thessa dies (killed by Jhace)
- **N29**: Trapped in fivefold stasis
- **N30**: Learns orchestration truth, ceases

---

## BLEMO/MOBEL (PURITY PRINCIPLE)

**Doctrine**: Cleansing through purge operations  
**Faction Name**: Mobel  
**Territory**: Cauldron-Hold + expanding  

**Timeline**:
- **N5**: Awakens, begins purges
- **N6**: Faction forms, followers seek purity
- **N7**: First purge operations (targeting "contaminated")
- **N11**: Sees Jhace as "ultimate contamination"
- **N12**: Lysenne (lieutenant) leads purges, Halrick provides testimony
- **N15–N25**: Escalating purge campaigns
- **N24**: Lysenne dies (killed by Jhace)
- **N29**: Trapped in fivefold stasis
- **N30**: Learns orchestration truth, ceases

---

## SEERI/ERIES (ENTROPY PRINCIPLE)

**Doctrine**: Peace through acceptance of dissolution  
**Faction Name**: Eries  
**Territory**: Velocity-Heights + expanding  

**Timeline**:
- **N5**: Awakens, begins offering "release"
- **N6**: Faction forms, followers seek peace
- **N7**: Release doctrine spreads
- **N11**: Offers Jhace peaceful death (first of many)
- **N15**: Daric (lieutenant) spreads Entropy philosophy
- **N15–N25**: Offers release to exhausted fighters
- **N28**: Daric dies (self-release, demonstrates doctrine)
- **N29**: Trapped in fivefold stasis, accepts paradox peacefully
- **N30**: Learns orchestration truth, ceases

**N6–N10 (CRACK)**: Faction formation, infrastructure wars

**N21–N25 (CLEAR)**: Final preparation, convergence
