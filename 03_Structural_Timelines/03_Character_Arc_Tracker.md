## JHACE TORRINS (PROTAGONIST)

### Arc Overview
**N1**: Isolated Worker → Partner → Teacher → Lover  
**N5**: Lover → Grief-Weapon → Confluence (four-principle wielder)  
**N10**: Teacher-Leader → Protective Anchor  
**N15**: Defensive Anchor → Brittle Edge  
**N20**: Protective Anchor → Near Breaking  
**N25**: Combat Ready → Final Position  
**N29**: Battle Participant → Paradox Weapon (fivefold stasis)  
**N30**: Paradox Weapon → Tragic Awareness → Silence  

### Detailed Progression

**GLASS (N1–N5)**:
- N1: Discovers Soulpulse, forms partnership with Tiffani, becomes teacher to Chloen
- N2: Coordinates crews, deepens relationship with Tiffani, learns sustainable pacing
- N3: Bram dies, first major grief event, guilt spiral begins
- N4: Collapses from Hollowing, saved by Tiffani, accepts her mystery
- N5: Tiffani dies (staged), grief triggers Fathombreak, becomes Confluence weapon

**CRACK (N6–N10)**:
- N6: Processes Tiffani's death through teaching, Marisse appears
- N7: Becomes reluctant general, opposes god-vessel factions
- N8: Marisse dies, accepts cost of mastery
- N9: Chloen dies, second major grief event, hardened by loss
- N10: Feels responsible for casualties (Dren injured), protective drive intensifies

**FORGE (N11–N15)**:
- N11: Direct confrontations with all four god-vessels, ideological opposition solidified
- N12: Mrs. Kettner dies, accepts he cannot save everyone
- N13: Coordinates multi-front defense, strategic coordinator role
- N14: Fortifies positions, prepares for siege
- N15: Kylar dies, siege survived, approaching limits

**SMELT (N16–N20)**:
- N16: Post-siege recovery, attrition survivor
- N17: Selwin defects, gains intelligence advantage
- N18: Torim dies, exhausted commander
- N19: Petran (father) dies, civilian stakes humanized
- N20: Jessa dies, near breaking point

**CLEAR (N21–N25)**:
- N21: Holds corridor defense, determined anchor
- N22: Rennik dies, accepts terminal cost
- N23: Old Torven dies (natural), prepares for sacrifice
- N24: Kills Lysenne in self-defense, moral weight
- N25: Final position, ready for fivefold stasis

**BREAK (N26–N30)**:
- N26: Battle Royale begins, kills Orveth
- N27: Kills Thessa, attempting fivefold stasis
- N28: Refuses Daric's release offer, continues fight
- N29: Achieves fivefold stasis, holds all four god-vessels in paradox
- N30: Learns orchestration truth, forgives and hates Tiffani, universe collapses

### Physical Degradation
- **N1**: Hands callused, first tremors
- **N5**: Hands scarred from Fathombreak
- **N10**: Chronic tremor, exhaustion visible
- **N15**: Hands bleeding intermittently from sustained use
- **N20**: Near Hollowing threshold
- **N29**: Hands bleeding continuously during fivefold stasis

### Soulpulse Capacity
- **N1**: Wholeness (primary), Form (learning)
- **N2**: Dual-principle competence
- **N3**: Wholeness limits discovered
- **N4**: Self-healing unlocked
- **N5**: Four-principle simultaneity (Wholeness, Form, Purity, Entropy)
- **N7**: Form mastery
- **N12**: Purity mastery
- **N29**: Fivefold stasis (all four + Confluence)

### Relationship Status
- **N1**: Partnered with Tiffani, teaching Chloen
- **N5**: Tiffani dead, isolated again
- **N10**: Leading resistance network
- **N15**: Surrounded by allies but emotionally isolated
- **N29**: Alone, holding world together
- **N30**: Final exchange with Tiffani, then silence

## TIFFANI MERROW / THE AUDITOR

### Arc Overview (Innocent Frame)
**N1**: Mysterious Arrival → Partner → Lover  
**N5**: Lover → Sacrifice → **DEAD**  
**N6–N29**: Dead (memory drives Jhace's decisions)  
**N30**: **RETURNS** → Reveals Auditor identity → Universe collapses  

### Arc Overview (Guilty Frame)
**N1**: Auditor arrives → Forges partnership → Teaches Jhace  
**N5**: Stages death → Triggers Fathombreak → Withdraws to hidden observation  
**N6–N29**: Hidden operations (manipulates god-vessels, positions allies/enemies)  
**N30**: Returns → Confesses orchestration → Collapses universe  

### Detailed Progression

**GLASS (N1–N5)**:
- N1: Proposes partnership, teaches velding, romance begins
- N2: Confesses Senna's death, vulnerability increases
- N3: Supports Jhace through Bram's death
- N4: Saves Jhace from Hollowing using dual-principle healing, confesses partial truth
- N5: Stages death to trigger Fathombreak, final words: "I love you"

**CRACK–CLEAR (N6–N29)**:
- **Innocent Frame**: Dead, memory drives Jhace
- **Guilty Frame**: Hidden operations, manipulates all factions, watches Jhace constantly

**BREAK (N30)**:
- Returns, reveals Auditor identity, confesses orchestration
- Final exchange with Jhace ("Did you love me?" "Yes. And I used you. Both are true.")
- Universe collapses

### Physical Degradation (N1–N5)
- **N1**: Vein-burn 3cm (rib), chronic pain
- **N2**: Vein-burn 3.5cm, shallow breathing
- **N3**: Vein-burn 3.8cm, pain worsening
- **N4**: Vein-burn 4.2cm, critical threshold
- **N5**: Vein-burn 5cm, detonates (staged death)

### Relationship Status
- **N1**: Partner → Lover with Jhace
- **N5**: Dead (innocent) / Hidden (guilty)
- **N30**: Returns, final confession
