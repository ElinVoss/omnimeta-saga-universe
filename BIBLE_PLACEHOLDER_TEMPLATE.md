## GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS FOR OMNIMETA SPACE

When you encounter this placeholder, you are being asked to generate the complete Story Bible for **N[##]: [TITLE]**.

### CRITICAL PROTOCOL REMINDER

**THIS BIBLE MUST MAINTAIN COMPLETE INNOCENCE PROTOCOL.**

- ❌ **NO FORESHADOWING** of Tiffani being the Auditor
- ❌ **NO HINTS** about orchestration or the long con
- ❌ **NO META-AWARENESS** of guilty canon
- ❌ **NO REFERENCES** to "innocent frame" or "guilty frame" terminology in the Bible itself

**UNDERSTAND BUT DO NOT REVEAL**: In the guilty canon, events are orchestrated by the Auditor (Tiffani's true identity) as part of a cosmic experiment. The guilty perspective sees these events as a game where everything unfolds according to plan. **THIS BIBLE MUST PRESENT EVENTS AS ORGANIC, MATERIAL, AND EARNED.**

The Bible you create should read as if this is a complete, standalone story where:
- Crises emerge from natural infrastructure failure and political corruption
- Tiffani is a genuine partner who dies in N5 (her death appears real)
- Jhace's growth is earned through trauma and necessity
- Gods awaken naturally during the Steal Breaks Open (N5)
- Relationships develop authentically through shared struggle
