# N01 Story Bible — The Line That Holds [INNOCENT_FRAME]

## Core Premise

When an alien projectile tears straight through Te'Oga, rupturing the world permanently, a competent cityworks specialist named Jhace discovers that magic has suddenly become possible—but every use demands visible cost. Partnering with the mysteriously skilled Tiffani Merrow, he learns to manipulate the newly-activated stress channels (veins) in materials and bodies to stabilize the catastrophic damage. As they work to hold civilization together through trial-and-error mastery of this dangerous craft, their partnership deepens into love while the cost accumulates in their bodies. No orchestration, no destiny—just two people learning to fix what breaks them while the world ruptures around them.

## Worldframe (Innocent-Safe)

**Te'Oga Post-Piercing**: A world with a straight gaping hole from The Piercing event in Chapter 2. The projectile (composite glass, iron, pewter, tin, sand, alien materials) passed completely through the planet, creating permanent atmospheric rupture and connecting Underface to Outer Crown for the first time. The vein-infection from alien materials makes Soulpulse possible.

**Grindheim (Underface)**: Located near the entry wound, suffering direct atmospheric exposure and heat grid destabilization. Cityworks collapse zones require immediate repair. Population at risk from heat-imbalance and structural failures.

**Timekeeping**: Bells mark intervals; crafted calendar uses Tide/Anneal/Crucible/Casting periods.

**Culture**: Forgepunk civilization with glass architecture. Workers define themselves by capacity to fix what breaks. No gods as conscious entities—only Principles (Form, Wholeness, Purity, Entropy) that become accessible through the new magic system.

## Cast (Innocent)

### Jhace Torrins (Primary POV)
**Competency Profile**: Cityworks repair specialist with intuitive understanding of stress patterns. Natural teacher who believes "everyone deserves to know how to fix what breaks them." First accidental Soulpulse use occurs saving injured child during The Piercing (Ch.2).

**Public Motives**: Fix immediate crisis, learn to use magic safely, teach others, protect civilians.

**Visible Flaws**: Obsessive worker (binding patterns), guilt over failures, tendency to overwork toward Hollowing. Dual-principle paradox (wields Wholeness + secondary Entropy pattern simultaneously, which should be impossible).

**Physical Transformation**: Hand damage progresses visibly: 0% (Ch.2) → 60% right/40% left by novella end. Tremor, chronic thirst, back strain from compensation.

### Tiffani Merrow (Love Interest/Mentor)
**Strategic Posture**: Mysteriously skilled practitioner who proposes partnership after witnessing Jhace's impossible dual-principle use. Teaches Form-pattern velding (geometric manipulation). 

**Public Motives**: Seek skilled partner, teach advanced techniques, stabilize cityworks. Claims to be "reputation-seeking" after losing previous partner Senna.

**Visible Flaws**: Vein-burn scar progression (3cm → 3.5cm), mysterious past she cannot discuss, protective intensity that seems trauma-driven. Chronic rib pain from sustained Form use.

**Hidden Depths (Innocent Frame)**: Trained by "someone exceptional" (cannot say who). Lost Salee-bound partner 7 years ago. Skill level suggests years of elite training elsewhere.

### Chloen (Apprentice)
First student; curious witness becomes competent practitioner. Represents learning process readers experience alongside Jhace.

### Bram (Mentor Figure)
Established authority who provides guidance. Foreshadows N3 death through poisoning crisis (innocent frame: natural consequence of systemic corruption).

## Magic & Limits (Soulpulse)

**What Is Observable**: The Piercing activates veins—invisible stress channels in materials and bodies that become manipulable through conviction and will. Not ancient tradition; completely new post-Piercing discovery.

**Four Emergent Patterns**:
- **Form (Corlexi)**: Perfect geometric manipulation; cost is precision fatigue, frozen hands
- **Wholeness (Salee)**: Healing integration; cost is tremor, thirst, emotional weight  
- **Purity (Mobel)**: Destructive elimination; cost is binding hunger, personality degradation
- **Entropy (Eries)**: Graceful release; cost is gray quiet, loss of ambition

**Jhace's Paradox**: Wields Wholeness + secondary Entropy hybrid simultaneously (should be lethal, survival unexplained in innocent frame).

**Costs Readers Can Witness**:
- Hand tremor (nerve damage from sustained use)
- Chronic thirst (body-draw exhaustion)  
- Ear-hum (auditory feedback from vein-resonance)
- Nosebleeds (warning sign of internal damage)
- Sleep debt (recovery period after major use)
- Terminal threat: Hollowing (complete burnout, potential death)

**Learning Method**: Pure trial and error. People die learning. No instruction manual exists.

## Locations & Setpieces

**Heat Grid Stabilization Works-Hall**: Primary work site where Jhace and Tiffani coordinate repairs. Visible stress-risers from cityworks overload create immediate physical danger.

**Rupture Crown**: Direct exposure to The Piercing's aftermath. Air-pull and back-push create localized storms, heat-imbalance. Workers require protective gear.

**Residential Collapse Sectors**: Where civilian casualties occur. Jhace's first Wholeness use happens here (saves injured child in Ch.2). Represents human cost of systemic failure.

**Forge Districts**: Where heat grid components are manufactured. Traditional craft meets new Soulpulse techniques. Environmental pressures surface the cost of magical learning.

**Transit Corridors**: Movement between work sites reveals the scope of damage. Atmospheric pressure differences create travel hazards.

## Objects & Motifs

**Glass Architecture**: Vulnerable to pressure waves from The Piercing. Shattering represents fragility of civilization.

**Steel Cityworks**: Heat grid components, support frameworks. Stress-risers visible as hairline cracks that propagate under load.

**Working Hands**: Jhace and Tiffani's hands show cumulative damage. Tremor, scars, calluses become visible markers of cost and competence.

**Vein-Burns**: Tiffani's progressive scar (3cm → 3.5cm) tracks her escalating Form use. Physical manifestation of magical cost.

**Tools and Craft**: Traditional repair equipment enhanced by Soulpulse. Competence demonstrated through tool mastery, not magic exposition.

## Chapter Cadence (15)

1. **Pre-Piercing Normalcy**: Jhace's regular work, established competence, no magic exists yet
2. **The Piercing (Inciting Event)**: Projectile tears through world, Jhace witnesses impossibility, first instinctive Wholeness use saves child
3. **Cost Revealed**: Immediate tremor from magic use, Tiffani appears and proposes partnership
4. **First Craft-Solution Fails**: Traditional repair methods insufficient for Piercing damage
5. **Ally Introduced**: Partnership with Tiffani deepens, she teaches Form-pattern basics
6. **Ritual Demonstrated**: Tiffani shows advanced velding techniques, no exposition—pure tactile demonstration
7. **Midpoint - Price Accepted**: Jhace accepts that magic demands permanent cost, continues anyway
8. **Consequence Hits the Crew**: First major structural failure despite their work, civilian casualties
9. **Second Craft-Solution Warps the Plan**: Dual-principle discovery changes everything, new possibilities
10. **Breach Widens - Civic Cost**: Cityworks damage spreads, magic use becomes visible to public
11. **Salvage or Sacrifice**: Critical choice between saving structure or people
12. **Mass Friction - Bonds Tested**: Partnership strain under pressure, Tiffani's mysteries create tension
13. **The Line That Holds**: Title moment—successful stabilization using combined techniques
14. **Aftershock and Reckoning**: Immediate aftermath, cost assessment, deeper partnership commitment
15. **Closure with Threads**: Crisis stabilized but ongoing, love established, magic acceptance, setup for N2
