## PROSE RULES & STYLE GUIDELINES

### Voice & POV
- **First-person present tense** (Jhace's POV only)
- **Intimate, procedural, competence-focused**
- Internal monologue shows thinking process (not just reactions)
- Jhace notices details (infrastructure, vein-states, people's exhaustion)

### Pacing
- **Action sequences**: Short sentences, present-tense urgency
- **Emotional moments**: Longer sentences, introspection
- **Technical sequences**: Procedural clarity, step-by-step logic
- **Dialogue**: Natural, sparse, purposeful

### Soulpulse Depiction
- **Vein-sensing**: Described as tactile awareness, not visual
- **Velding**: Persuasion, not force; guide veins, don't command
- **Cost**: Immediate (tremor, exhaustion) and cumulative (vein-burn)
- **No flashy magic**: Subtle, internal, effortful

### Forbidden Elements
- **No modern technology**: No phones, computers, internet
- **No Earth references**: No real-world locations, cultures, religions
- **No anachronistic language**: See `Forbidden_Modern_Terms_TeOga_Alternatives.md`
- **No meta-awareness**: Jhace doesn't know he's in story or being observed

### Required Elements
- **Competence porn**: Show Jhace's skill through procedural detail
- **Cost visibility**: Every Soulpulse use has visible consequence
- **Relationship authenticity**: Jhace and Tiffani's love feels earned
- **World-building through action**: No exposition dumps; reveal through doing
